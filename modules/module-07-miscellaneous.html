L1 Licensing Overview

Understanding how Document Understanding and IXP are licensed helps you plan your automation projects and avoid unexpected costs.

DU Licensing Models

License TypeUnitKey Limits
Community EditionNo unit display on Licenses page2 pages/4MB per doc, 50 requests/hour, data stored for QA
Enterprise EditionPages (shown on Licenses page)20 megapixels/page, 10MB per doc (JSON-Base64), data NOT stored
Public Endpoints (all)PagesMax 30 pages per document via public endpoint; deploy as ML Skill for more

What Consumes Licenses?

  • Page-based consumption: Each page processed through DU extraction consumes license units
  • Modern Projects: Do NOT consume AI units for training or model serving — infrastructure is managed by UiPath
  • Classic Projects / AI Center: Consume AI units for model training pipelines and ML Skill serving
  • IXP Complex Documents: Uses a separate consumption model — check your license agreement
💡
Cost Optimization Tip: For high-volume workflows, pre-filter documents before OCR to avoid processing pages you don't need. For example, if you only need page 1 of a 50-page document, split it first.

L2 DU Use Case Examples

Finance & Accounts Payable

  • Invoice Processing: Extract vendor, amount, line items from invoices; post to ERP (SAP, Oracle)
  • Purchase Order Matching: Match POs against invoices for 3-way matching
  • Expense Report Processing: Extract receipt data for reimbursement workflows

Human Resources

  • Resume Screening: Extract candidate info from CVs for ATS upload
  • Onboarding Document Processing: Extract data from employment forms, ID documents
  • Offer Letter Generation: Extract from templates to generate personalized letters

Legal & Compliance

  • Contract Analysis: Extract key clauses, dates, parties from contracts
  • Due Diligence: Process large volumes of legal documents for M&A
  • Regulatory Submissions: Extract and validate data from regulatory forms

Healthcare

  • Claims Processing: Extract claim data from insurance forms
  • Patient Record Digitization: Extract structured data from medical records
  • Prescription Processing: Extract medication details from prescription forms

Banking & Insurance

  • Loan Application Processing: Extract applicant data from loan applications
  • KYC Document Processing: Extract ID, address verification from identity documents
  • Insurance Policy Intake: Extract coverage details from policy documents
