What is UiPath IXP?

UiPath Intelligent Extraction Processing (IXP) is UiPath's unified intelligent document processing (IDP) platform. It enables organizations to extract, classify, and process data from virtually any type of document at enterprise scale — using a combination of traditional ML, specialized AI, and generative AI.

IXP = Three Capabilities in One Platform

IXP brings together three major capabilities under a single product in Automation Cloud:

  • Document Understanding (DU) — Extract data from structured & semi-structured documents (forms, invoices, purchase orders, etc.)
  • Generative Extraction for Unstructured & Complex Documents — Use generative AI to extract data from high-complexity, varied-structure documents (contracts, reports, lease agreements)
  • Communications Mining (CM) — Extract intent, sentiment, and context from emails, tickets, and messages

IXP Capabilities Deep Dive

Document Understanding (DU)

Document Understanding enables you to extract relevant data from structured and semi-structured documents — documents that follow a consistent layout or format. DU is available as a standalone capability in Automation Cloud, Automation Suite, and Standalone deployments.

  • Supports OCR (UiPath, Google, Microsoft, ABBYY engines)
  • Classification: keyword-based, ML-based, or intelligent hybrid
  • Extraction: rule-based (RegEx, Form Extractor), ML-based (out-of-the-box and custom)
  • Human-in-the-loop validation via Action Center
  • Modern experience with Active Learning for fast model training

IXP for Complex & Unstructured Documents

This capability uses Generative AI to extract data from documents that have complex tables, free-form text, varying layouts, or inferred values not explicitly present. It is particularly powerful for:

Contracts Lease Agreements Legal Documents Brokerage Statements Insurance Policies Medical Reports Engineering Specs

Key differentiators of IXP for Complex Docs:

  • Generative AI with prompt-based extraction (no large labeled datasets needed to start)
  • Rapid prototyping — see predictions immediately without training
  • Built-in ground truth annotation for model evaluation
  • Model versioning, performance comparison, and deployment management
  • Supports documents up to 150 pages
💡 Tip
DU vs IXP for Complex Docs: Use Document Understanding for structured/semi-structured docs (invoices, forms). Use IXP Complex Docs for documents with <150 pages that have complex tables, graphics, free-form text, inferred values, or 'packet' documents with multiple document types.

Communications Mining (CM)

Communications Mining extracts key intent, sentiment, and context data from messages and communications. Best suited for high-volume email and ticket processing (50k+ messages for best UX & ROI). It provides:

  • Annotation & Validation UI with confidence scores
  • Retraining for continuous improvement
  • Analytics and insights dashboards

Choosing the Right IXP Capability

Understanding which IXP capability to use for your use case is critical for success. Here is a decision guide:

Reports / Contracts >150 pagesDU Generative Extraction (RAG)Built-in RAG for long docs
Email classification & intentCommunications MiningOptimized for high-volume comms
Legacy/structured, high volumeDU Classic (Workflow)Low AI tolerance, rule-based control
📌 Note
IXP is the umbrella: When someone says "IXP," they are referring to the full platform. Document Understanding and Communications Mining are components within IXP. In day-to-day usage, "IXP for complex documents" usually refers to the generative extraction capability for unstructured docs.
